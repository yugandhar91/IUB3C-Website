Events

Each semester 3C presents at least one event for members to learn about Chinese culture. Typically, in the fall semester, the event is held to celebrate the Mid-Autumn Festival. In the spring semester, the event is held to welcome the spring season. During the event, members will be able to enjoy varies traditional Chinese activities such as tea art, paper-cutting and bamboo dance. Traditional Chinese performance will also be presented. Besides, 3C also participates in events such as Chinese table and Lotus Blossoms World Bazaar.

Workshops

Chinese Calligraphy Club (3C) IUB will have calligraphy workshop every week and hold in-club calligraphy competition per semester. All IU students and any calligraphy scripts are welcomed. Join us and have fun.

In Spring 2015, Chinese Calligraphy Club continues to present free Calligraphy Workshop to you. Our mentors will always be with us and all skill level are welcome! Brush pens and papers are available here.
